Meet the Sanrio Characters

Hello Kitty

Type: Anthropomorphic cat-girl (officially a little girl). Created in 1974, she wears a red bow and lives in London. Known for her kindness and iconic lack of a mouth so people can project their own feelings onto her.

My Melody

Type: Rabbit. Debuted in 1975, she wears a pink (or red) hood. She’s sweet, kind, and loves baking. Best friends with Hello Kitty.

Kuromi

Type: Rabbit. Rebellious and mischievous, but with a softer side. Wears a black jester’s hat with a pink skull. Born on Halloween (Oct 31).

Cinnamoroll

Type: Puppy. Born March 6, 2002 on a cloud. With big ears that let him fly and a cinnamon roll-like tail, he’s gentle and very cute.

Little Twin Stars

Type: Twin angels. Kiki and Lala were born on December 24 and are often seen in pastel celestial settings.

Keroppi

Type: Frog. Lives at Donut Pond, adventurous and cheerful. Has siblings and many friends like Koroppi and Pikki.

Pompompurin

Type: Golden Retriever. Introduced in 1996, he wears a brown beret and loves pudding. Relaxed, friendly, and lovable.

Pochacco

Type: Dog. Energetic and sporty, he’s often shown in active outfits and loves playing games with friends.

Badtz-Maru

Type: Penguin. A bit sassy and mischievous with a rebellious streak. Born April 1, he’s cheeky but lovable.
