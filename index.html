About Sanrio

Sanrio is also known for bringing people together through shared love of kawaii culture. Fans around the world connect at Sanrio-themed events, pop-up shops, and even amusement parks like Puroland in Japan, where the characters step off the page and into real life. Each experience is designed to create smiles and unforgettable memories, proving that Sanrio is not just for kids, but for anyone who believes in the magic of playfulness and positivity.

With a legacy spanning over 60 years, Sanrio continues to evolve while staying true to its mission of “small gift, big smile.” From timeless icons to fresh new characters, Sanrio keeps finding ways to spark joy in daily life. Whether you’re looking for adorable collectibles, heartwarming gifts, or just a dose of happiness, Sanrio offers a world where cuteness and kindness always shine bright.
