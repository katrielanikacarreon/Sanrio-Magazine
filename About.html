About the Author

Meet the Author: Hello! My name is Katriel Anika D. Carreon from 9-Arayat. I created this website about Sanrio because I’ve always loved the creativity, happiness, and charm that these characters bring. My personal favorite character is My Melody because she is sweet, caring, and always ready to help her friends, which inspires me to be kind and thoughtful in my own life. Through this project, I wanted to show not only my favorite characters but also how Sanrio teaches important values like friendship, helping others, and staying cheerful even in small everyday moments. Creating this website has been fun and exciting, and I’m proud to share a piece of my love for Sanrio with everyone who visits.
